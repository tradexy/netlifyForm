Drainage Solutions from Leaks Plumbing

Blocked drains, toilets, blocked sinks, sewers and foul smells can be some of the most disconcerting things for house-owners or commercial property owners to have to deal with. It is rarely a job that is relished and quite often something that could have been prevented in the first place. At Leaks Plumbing we take our drainage service and solutions very seriously.

Residential and Commercial

Leaks Plumbing offers a range of plumbing and drainage services, both domestic and commercial. We can repair drains that are broken or have collapsed. We can unblock your drains, reline and patch them, carry out professional mechanical descaling as well as drain jetting. We use CCTV surveys to ascertain the problem and can even replace drains entirely if required, though this is usually a last resort.

Central heating and boilers

Leaks plumbers are specialist plumbing and heating engineers. Whether you have a boiler breakdown emergency, or simply need a new boiler installed, we are on hand to help. We can install central heating systems, replace or repair boilers, wall mount radiators and repair and install gas pipework, among many other things.
